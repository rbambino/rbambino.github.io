Raffael Kind
PhD Candidate in Economics

Welcome to my website!

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the European University Institute in Florence, Italy. My research focuses on labor economics, intergenerational social mobility and inequality, and political economy. I combine applied microeconometric techniques to identify causal relationships with theoretical macroeconomic modeling to deepen the understanding of the mechanisms underlying my empirical findings. I work under the supervision of Alexander Monge-Naranjo and Fabrizia Mealli. Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed a Master’s degree in Economics at the University of Konstanz, Germany.
